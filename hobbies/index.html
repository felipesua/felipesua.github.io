Hobbies

Biking

Mountain biking awesome in the Boston area. I visit the Middlesex fells and the Cutler park reservation often. I also frequently road bike to the Walden Pond and the Blue Hills reservation.

Rock climbing

My foray into this beautiful sport started in 2019 while being a PhD student at MIT. I took part in the 2021 School of Rock organized by the MIT outing club.

I am mostly a gym boulderer (V4 flash, V6-7 max) and climb sport routes occasionally (5.10-5.11c).
